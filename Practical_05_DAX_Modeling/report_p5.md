Placeholder report for the DAX modeling practical.

## 1. What is the difference between a column and a measure?
Answer:
A column stores data for every row in a table, while a measure performs calculations (such as sum, average, or count) based on the current filters and is calculated only when needed.

## 2. Which KPI would you put on top of the dashboard and why?
Answer:
I would place Total Sales at the top of the dashboard because it is the most important business KPI and gives a quick overview of overall business performance.
