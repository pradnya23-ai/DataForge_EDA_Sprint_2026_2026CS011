
## 1.What does one row represent?
Answer: One row represents a single record/observation (e.g., one customer, transaction, or event depending on the dataset).

## 2. Which columns look numerical, categorical and date/time?
Answer:
Numerical columns: Sales, Quantity, Discount, Profit 
Categorical columns: Category, Sub-Category, Region, Segment, Customer Name
Date/Time column: Order Date

## 3. What can go wrong if a team starts analysis without a data dictionary?
Answer:
Without a data dictionary, people may misunderstand the columns, use the wrong data, and make incorrect analysis and business decisions.
