## 1. What cleaning decision could affect business conclusions?
Answer:
I filled missing numeric values with the median value instead of deleting the rows. This keeps more data for analysis, but it can slightly change the final results like averages and totals.

The Order Month column is the most useful because it shows sales month by month. It helps us find which months have higher or lower sales and makes it easier to see sales trends.
